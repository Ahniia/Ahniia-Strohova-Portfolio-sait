Dating App

Overview

This app, was a learning project that I created during my studies. But I consider it successful because it was my first, almost full-fledged project. Later I took this project as a basis for a real mobile application for organizing conferences

My Role

Sole UX/UI Designer

Duration

2 weeks

Tools

Figma, Colour Contrast Analyser,Â  Adobe Illustrator, Excel, UsabilityHub, Paper & Pencil

Grids

I started working on the project by defining the overall structure of the layout of the mobile app, namely building the grids I would use to create my project.

Prototypes

After defining the grid, I moved on to work on the prototype application. I defined the screens I would be working on and how they would interact with each other. Also, I decided what the right part of the screens would be, and the bottom active part. After that, I moved on to building the layouts.

Colors

Then, I moved on to picking out the color combinations I would use in my project. I had to find two color schemes, one for the light theme and one for the dark mode. In addition, I determined the color scheme for the illustrations. These illustrations were found by me on the internet as free-use illustration elements. I assembled them into compositions and changed the color scheme to match the design of my application.

Design

After that, I put it all together. I gave color to my mobile app mockups, also fixed various flaws, roughnesses that were in my mockups, added illustrations. And finally, I got the finished mobile app design in dark and light mode which you can see below
